## 简介

Adithyavairavan Murali 是 NVIDIA 的研究员，专注于 GPU-accelerated 机器人运动规划和操控。他是 cuRobo 和 cuRoboV2 的核心作者之一。

## 代表工作

- cuRoboV2: Dynamics-aware motion generation with depth-fused distance fields for high-DoF robots (2026)
- cuRobo: GPU-accelerated motion generation for robot arms (ICRA 2023)
- CABINET: Learning scalable, task-relevant robot manipulation from internet videos (2023)
