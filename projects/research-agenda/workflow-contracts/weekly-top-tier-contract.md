# Weekly Top-Tier Contract

## Purpose

The weekly review is a top-tier pressure test over the last 7 days of raw, promoted, parked, rewrite, and rescue candidates. It is a triage report, not an automatic promotion system.

## Inputs

- Daily Gemini greenhouse JSON files.
- Daily agenda deltas.
- Daily Codex review reports when present.
- Existing seed titles.
- Local novelty-pressure hits.

## Rubric

Weekly review uses a second-layer rubric:

- `breakthrough_potential`
- `engineering_pathology_strength`
- `non_obvious_mechanism`
- `baseline_survivability`
- `killer_experiment_clarity`
- `venue_fit`
- `rescue_value`

## Allowed Conclusions

- `push_to_user_review`
- `rewrite_for_mechanism`
- `park_for_weekly_followup`
- `reject_with_rescue`

## Required Sections

The weekly report must contain:

- Executive Verdict
- Top-Tier Adversarial Review
- Novelty Pressure
- Strongest Baseline
- Killer Experiment Plan
- Venue Fit
- Creativity Preservation
- Next 7 Days

## Red Lines

- Do not move or delete idea folders.
- Do not claim confirmed novelty.
- Do not promote candidates automatically.
- Do not bury a mechanism-sharp but risky candidate just because its daily score was low.
